What is the Turtle Cam?

The Turtle Cam is a budget-friendly, waterproof camera designed to be securely attached to freshwater turtles, allowing researchers to observe their daily activities in their natural habitat. Its programmable features let you schedule video recordings or image captures at customizable intervals and durations. Additionally, the camera includes a delayed start function, providing ample time for setup before recording begins.

Why We Created the Turtle Cam

The Turtle Cam was developed to support research efforts focused on the preservation of freshwater turtles. By recording their behaviors, movement patterns, and mating habits, researchers can gain valuable insights that contribute to conservation strategies and habitat protection efforts.
